Overview

Designing across a diverse client ecosystem

At Alkime, I work across a range of client engagements — delivering seamless user experiences across web and mobile platforms for their clients and their customers. Each project brings its own unique design challenges, user contexts, and business objectives.

My role spans the full design process: from initial discovery and research through to interaction design, prototyping, and handoff. I collaborate closely with product and engineering teams to ensure what gets shipped is both user-centered and technically grounded.

Challenge

Adapting quickly across varied contexts

Working across multiple client engagements simultaneously requires a high degree of adaptability. Each client brings different brand guidelines, user needs, technical constraints, and team dynamics — the challenge is maintaining design quality and coherence while moving fluidly between these contexts.

Additionally, delivering experiences that feel genuinely native to each client's product — rather than templated or generic — requires deep listening and fast context-switching.
